Our Purpose

CodeSeed Club was created to give beginners a simple and welcoming place to learn programming. The goal is to help students build confidence while learning the basics of coding.

Founder

The club was founded by Saketh Chodisetti, a high school student who enjoys working with technology and helping others learn. He started CodeSeed Club to share what he has learned and encourage new students to explore programming.

What We Teach

  • Python
  • Basics of Object‑Oriented Programming (OOP)
  • Scratch for Beginners
  • HTML Basics

Who Can Join?

Anyone who is new to coding is welcome. No experience is required—just curiosity and a willingness to learn.
